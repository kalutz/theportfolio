My name is Katharina Lutz.

I am a media design student at the MD.H in Munich (Germany) where I began my studies in 2019. Currently I am in the middle of my fourth semester.

Since 2020 I am also a working student at MD.H, where my tasks revolve around marketing projects, corporate identity, advertisement and social media representation.

Throughout my studies I was/am able to gain additional experience in — fascinating to me — hand-set type by performing multiple tasks in the university's print workshop. Since the first semester I have happily used the opportunity to realize my own ideas there as well.

While the computer is my main tool for design work currently, I do like working without it. That is why I spend my free time either drawing with pen and paper or painting. Since I believe that trying out new things is the best way to learn and grow, I like to challenge myself by trying new approaches. I live for everything that means experimenting.

Usually I am busy working on my graphic design projects. While I enjoy making creations by myself, I find great enthusiasm in teamwork, as it can spice things up by introducing different perspectives and ideas.

I have always been a creative person, so it soon became clear to me that I wanted to work in the creative department in the future. Now I have found my passion in graphic design. I aspire to become a creator whose work has the potential to catch the eye, mind and heart.
