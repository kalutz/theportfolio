Nice to meet you!

My name is Katharina Lutz.
I am a media design student from the MD.H in Munich (Germany) where I began studying in 2019. Currently I am in the middle of my fourth semester, looking forward to ones to come before I hope to complete my studies with the degree "Bachelor of Arts".

Back in my school days I did not know what profession to choose. However, as I have always been a creative person, it soon became clear to me that I wanted to work in the creative department in the future.
Now I have found my passion in graphic design. So I aspire to become a creator whose work catches not only the eye but also the mind and heart.

One more thing about me:
I live for contrast and experimenting with typography.
