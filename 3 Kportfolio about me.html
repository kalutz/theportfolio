My name is Katharina Lutz.

I graduated from MD.H in Munich (Germany) as a bachelor of design in 2023. Motivated through my studies, I will soon be able to deepen my knowledge regarding movable type and traditional printing techniques thanks to the scholarship from the Verein für die Schwarze Kunst e.V.

By being a working student at MD.H in the marketing department for a year and working as a graphic designer at neomind design studio in Munich, I was able to expand my theoretical knowledge, gain useful experiences and broaden my horizons.

Although the computer is my main tool for design work currently, I do enjoy working without it.
I like to approach projects experimentally whenever possible.
