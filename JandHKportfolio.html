The experimental teaser film
featuring Jekyll and Hyde

ABOUT

The goal of the experimental film is to put the focus onto psychological human limits and complex mind in a dystopian way.

The famous scottish novel Strange Case of Dr. Jekyll and Mr. Hyde demonstrates how excessive striving might lead to dangerous outcomes for the individual.

The chemically induced transformation of the conscientious and likable character Dr. Jekyll into his psychotic counterpart Mr. Hyde becomes the abstract topic of the film.

To mess with the viewers eye and mind and to transport the mad atmosphere sounds and images come together experimentally and in a simple color scheme.

The colours blue and red come together as an important stylistic device and show the differences, confrontation and transformation of Dr. Jekyll and Mr. Hyde.

This film is built up with the help of simple objects.
Experiments with for example flashlights and ink-colored fluids were a part of the process.
