ARTIST's
VITA FLYER

The informative flyer
for a painter's exhibition.

Dagmar Anna Steidle is a painter from Germany. Some of her paintings can be found in Europe, Southfrica and the US, where she has travelled multiple times. Her inspiration comes from the beauty of nature and life experience. She finds beauty in every creation.

"The joy of painting runs through my life like a red thread."

Mrs Steidle wanted a flyer or booklet for a planned exhibition of her work. The exhibition includes many of her paintings of flowers which is why elements of those can be found on the flyer.

Since the client is a calm and gentle personality — like her paintings — the overall design was kept simple and classic in style. A variant of the font Garamond is used to accompany the big and smaller images in a quiet way as the focus is set onto the pictures. To keep everything in order and not distract from the images, text is kept on the images' baselines.

An exception to that can be found on the first inner, and last outer page that both come together in a quote and two different images. Anna Dagmar Steidle's life is shaped by many experiences that go into many directions and make her the person that she is today. Everything in life has multiple facettes and at least two sides, and we never know what time brings to us.

"Paths are created by walking them."

The two different images imply that there is more to her paintings just like in life. There is always a deeper meaning or a second outlook on things. The viewer is motivated to indulge in deeper thoughts.

Once the viewer opens the flyer which is formed as a wrap fold further, in its heart there is Mrs. Steidles curriculum vitae, wrapped in her paintings. To get to know Mrs. Steidle as a creative personality, quotes become an essential factor in the design of the flyer.

"Painting is to me like being able to capture this feeling of inner beauty, love and longing onto the canvas."

Her passion for what she does is located in the heart of the flyer, as well as one of her life experiences:

"Nobody knows what talents may be hidden inside until they try to bring them out."

Last, the backside of the flyer shows a portrait of Dagmar Anna Steidle — that aligns with the frontside's picture — alongside with summarized information and contact data.

This flyer was my first project for a private client.
Therefore it was essentially important to communicate well at all stages of the design process.

I am thankful to Mrs. Steidle for trusting me with this intimate project.
