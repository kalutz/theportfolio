LYSIS

To get a better understanding of typography and its characteristics
this book analyzes specifically the egyptienne font "Rockwell".

The book contains detailed examinations of each letter and glyph. There are pages comparing similar fonts and their characteristics as well as information about "Rockwell's" history.

To visualize contrasts and counter spaces for example, the book occasionally uses transparent pages that contribute to its unique feel as well.

The overall layout is kept simple but does make typographic statements and graphic elements. To bring the analysis to life there are various experimentally styled pages all over the book.


Like that the book "Rockwell" comes to life with its bold visuals. The unconventional colour green is found on several pages, purposefully highlighting specific elements.


This project was realized together with Celina Hofmann and Michaela Kappes.
